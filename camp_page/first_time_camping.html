My First Time Camping

Hey Y'all! This is my first blog post about starting my camping journey. This is my first time ever camping so I don't want to be judged for the decisions I made. We all settle into things differently and this is my journey. With that out of the way, my first time camping...*drumroll please*... was glamping in my boyfriend's backyard.

"What is glamping?" some of y'all might be asking. Well, let me tell you. Glamping is glamorous camping. Meaning you camp in a more glorious way than most would, whether that be in a camper, a cabin, with an air mattress, etc. My version of glamping was sleeping in a ten-person tent, with a queen-sized air mattress, pillows, and a power strip running from the house... so we could plug in fairy lights and charge his laptop to watch the first Twilight film.

My takeaway from this new experience is that you know yourself better than anyone else and that you shouldn't push yourself into foreign territory in a manner you aren't okay with. I highly recommend that you glamp for your first time. I cannot wait to keep y'all posted about more of my camping adventures!
