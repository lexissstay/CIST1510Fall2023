Rushing River

Hey Y'all! This adventure is about my first time camping near a natural body of water!!! One thing about me I have yet to mention is that I absolutely love water. Well, anything besides a lake. I just don't like how dirty the water can be and how stagnant it is. Either way, this was my first time camping by water and I was so excited for the peaceful sounds of the water rushing by.

This trip my boyfriend and I camped in a two-person tent right next to the river. The sounds were phenomenal and made me sleep like a baby. When we woke up we decided to take a swim in the river to wake up. And then finished the trip off with a long and lovely hike through the surrounding wilderness.

This has by far been my favorite camping trip and I will definitely be planning more destinations that involve water. This camping trip taught me the dangers of being near water which is mainly worrying about not being swept away, drowning, or interfering with hazardous wildlife like the water moccasin here in Georgia. I don't see how anyone could hate camping out near water, and this should definitely be something everyone should try. I cannot wait to go on more adventures and keep y'all updated and educated!
